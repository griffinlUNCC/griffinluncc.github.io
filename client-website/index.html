About Me

Alisha Zhao is a freshman at SCAD studying animation. Zhao is familiar with many mediums in the animation space, including 2D hand-drawn animation, stop-motion, digital character rigging, and motion graphics design. She has explored both traditional frame-by-frame techniques and modern software workflows, giving her a versatile foundation for storytelling through movement. Zhao is also a talented artist, familiar with figure drawing, color theory, storyboarding, and concept art development. Her sketchbooks reveal a particular interest in character design and environmental storytelling, often blending whimsical elements with grounded emotional narratives. Outside the studio, she draws inspiration from indie animated shorts, graphic novels, and the natural landscapes found in Georgia.
