Our Business

At Grateful Lizard, we believe that rainy days should never mean dull days. That’s why every rain hat we create is designed to add a splash of personality to your wet-weather wardrobe. Whether you're navigating a stormy sidewalk or dancing in festival drizzle, our hats are built to move with you—durable enough for real adventures, stylish enough to wear anywhere. Each collection is released in limited runs, so when you wear a Grateful Lizard hat, you’re not just staying dry—you’re standing out.

We're proud to be a small business with a big mission: to make gear that’s as kind to the planet as it is to your sense of style. All of our rain hats are ethically made in small batches, using sustainable practices and materials that leave a lighter footprint. From our biodegradable packaging to our local artist collaborations, every detail is thoughtfully considered. Explore our collection and find a hat that speaks to you—because staying dry should never mean blending in.
