Overall Review

The first issue I notice is that all links redirect to new pages. This should only be done for pages that lead to external sites, such as going from your ITIS-3135 to your Design Studio page, not your ITIS-3135 to the introduction page. There is also the issue of going from a "subpage," such as introduction, to another subpage. This is fixed by creating a different heading/footer template. This is seen in my site, as I ran into a similar issue.

As for your other sites, they again have strong design fundamentals that use a good color scheme and CRAP principles properly. However, there are some small issues. Mighty Chicken should not have a course link, and I cannot find the hobby site.

I think the sites are overall very well designed, and follow CRAP principles for the most part, with small misses listed above that don't apply to the design, just small development issues. The code and file organization is very well done and meets all standards. All expected sites and elements are present!
