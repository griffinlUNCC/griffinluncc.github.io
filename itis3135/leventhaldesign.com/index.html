Griffin Leventhal's Services

Griffin Leventhal Design Firm is a modern firm specializing in modernizing older sites. We excel in implementing design languages such as Material as well as custom or proprietary design langauges as well. We will help you transition older sites into sleek and modern variants that will boost sales and ensure easy maintenence for years to come.

Services Offered

  • Rapid Prototyping
  • Static Webpages
  • Website Design Schemes
  • Dynamic Webpages
  • Modern Redesigning

Clients and I go through a few stages. We start by strictly defining the goals of the client - ensuring we leave with a clear design stylesheet that can be used again in the future. We then go through an iterative design process in which we constantly refer to you for feedback until a final design is settled upon. Finally, we establish a plan for long-term support - whether thats designing it for maintenence for others or establishing a plan for regular maintenance and on-call support.
